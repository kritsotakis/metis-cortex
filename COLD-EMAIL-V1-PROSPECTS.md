## ICP Filtering — applied to every row

✅ **Solo or small team** — 1–10 employees, owner-operator decision-maker
✅ **Phone-driven leads** — phone number prominent on website
✅ **Active business** — last website update within 12 months (cross-check via copyright year, blog dates, Google reviews dated)
✅ **Sydney-based** — services Sydney metro, not just listed there
✅ **Owner contact reachable** — direct email for owner/manager publicly findable, NOT just `info@` or generic webform

❌ **No big franchises** — corporate buying authority sits with HQ, not the local owner; you can't close a Belle Property local office because corporate marketing already mandates their tools
❌ **No anyone in Peter's existing network** — those are warm intros, not cold; pull them out into a separate "warm" list
❌ **No "no-spam / no-solicitation" sites** — respect it, skip
❌ **No companies already on Sophiie / Chime Labs / Johnni AI / TransferToAI customer pages** — they're already buying competitor product

```
- Buying authority: owner / founder / managing director
- Budget signal: spending on Google Ads OR active website with regular updates

Output: 50 candidates, ranked by ICP fit, with verified emails + LinkedIn URLs + phone numbers.
```

Apollo will return owner names, verified emails, LinkedIn profiles, and confidence scores. The 30 starter rows above give you the brand/website context per company; Apollo fills in the human-level data.

If you'd rather skip Apollo and go manual, the alternate path:
1. For each company in the table, visit their website's "About" or "Team" page → identify owner/founder
2. LinkedIn search for that name + company → confirm role
3. Find email via [hunter.io](https://hunter.io) or guess pattern (firstname@domain.com.au is the most common AU SMB pattern)
4. Verify with [neverbounce.com](https://neverbounce.com) or [zerobounce.net](https://zerobounce.net) before sending

Manual takes ~15 min per row × 50 = 12.5 hours. Apollo: 5 minutes.

## Sending discipline

When you send (not now, not until DSK pilot data is real):

| Rule | Why |
|---|---|
| **Max 25 emails/day, 5 days/week** | Volume above this triggers spam filters on shared SMTP infrastructure (and Australian mail providers). 25/day × 50 prospects = 2 weeks of throughput. |
| **Send from `peter@kritsotakis.com.au`, NOT `hello@metiscortex.au`** | The mailbox at `hello@` is a forwarder for v1 (Task 2). Replies need to come back to a real inbox you control. Once Google Workspace is set up (Phase 2), switch to `peter@metiscortex.au`. |
| **Sending window: 09:00–11:00 AEST, Tue/Wed/Thu** | Highest open rate window for Australian B2B SMBs. Avoid Mondays (admin catch-up day) and Fridays (winding down). |
| **One follow-up after 5 business days, then stop** | A second email lifts response rate by ~30%. A third email lifts it by ~5% and burns goodwill. Two-touch sequence only. |
| **Track replies in a simple spreadsheet** | Don't pay for HubSpot / Apollo's email engine yet. Google Sheet with columns: name, sent date, reply Y/N, demo booked Y/N, status. Upgrade tooling at 100+ prospects. |
